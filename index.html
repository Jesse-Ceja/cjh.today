About

This site was created for all of the storytellers of the world. People who love writing, drawing, or expressing their stories in whatever format they feel most comfortable with. This is a safe space where you can either post your stories or view posts from others. Whether you want feedback or just want to share your work with others, it doesn't matter! Plus if you don't have a writing platform, you can do so here!
